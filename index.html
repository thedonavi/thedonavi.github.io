OUR STORY

Online shopping sites contain infinite brands and products. Navigating through those sites for hours to find a gift is hard & time consuming. So we gathered our team to put an end to this!

We created a simple, unique and easy gift recommendation engine for you, which recommends products of popular brands.

Donavi only asks you a couple of questions to tailor the perfect gift for your recipient - just sit back and enjoy the satisfaction of buying a good gift, the easy way.

OUR VISION

Everyone loves a well-thought gift. However, getting creative when choosing a gift is hard AND there are so many options to choose from... We don’t think it has to be that way.

We understand that you might be busy or undecisive when it comes to gifting. So, we decided to say no to a disorganized and overwhelming gifting site.

With Donavi, save time on choosing gifts! Deciding what to buy among products of popular brands is now easy. You’ll be out of here happy and satisfied in under 10 minutes.
